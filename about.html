About IFAM

Our Vision, Mission, and Values

Our Vision

To create a world where aspiring legal professionals have access to transformative opportunities in advocacy, mooting, and legal education, empowering them to lead the future of justice globally.

The International Forum for Advocacy and Mooting envisions becoming a premier institution for nurturing and enhancing mooting and dispute-resolution practices amongst law students and professionals by providing platforms for practical learning, such as moot court competitions, training programs, and global partnerships.

We aim to create a space where law students can become capable legal professionals with specialized training and support. Our ultimate goal is to foster and raise the mooting standards and resources in India and help students gain invaluable exposure to the global legal arena, ensuring they are equipped to handle real-world legal challenges.

We are dedicated to fostering advocacy excellence by hosting premier moot court competitions, providing expert training, and building international collaborations. Our mission is to break barriers and provide every law student the opportunity to thrive, represent, and excel on a global stage.

Our mission is to provide law students with the tools and opportunities to excel in mooting, arbitration, mediation, negotiation, and conciliation through practical, hands-on training and exposure to national and international competitions.

We are committed to promoting mooting as an essential skill in legal education, sponsoring and coaching teams for prestigious national and international competitions, and forming partnerships with global universities and institutions to facilitate international programs and exchanges.

The IFAM aims to foster a new generation of lawyers who are well-versed in alternative dispute resolution mechanisms and advocacy skills through targeted mentorship and collaboration with the best legal minds globally.

Our Objectives

Host Moot Court Competitions

Organize diverse national and international moots to simulate real-world legal challenges in areas like constitutional law, arbitration, and environmental law.

Foster Legal Training

Conduct specialized workshops on legal research, drafting, and oral advocacy with guidance from seasoned mooters, academics, and practitioners.

Sponsor and Support Talent

Provide financial aid, training, and resources to promising law students, ensuring financial barriers never limit potential.

Collaborate Globally

Build partnerships with international universities and organizations to facilitate cross-cultural learning and expertise sharing.

Enhance ADR Education

Promote alternative dispute resolution methods through specialized programs, competitions, and collaborations with dispute resolution bodies.

Recognize Achievements

Celebrate students who bring laurels to India through international events, encouraging excellence and leadership.

Dr. Srijan Mishra

President, International Forum for Advocacy and Mooting (IFAM)

Dr. Srijan Mishra is a distinguished legal academic, and mooting expert dedicated to advancing legal advocacy, dispute resolution, and legal education worldwide. As the President of the International Forum for Advocacy and Mooting (IFAM), he leads global initiatives aimed at enhancing mooting culture, strengthening advocacy skills, and fostering collaboration among legal professionals, scholars, and students. His vision for IFAM is to create an ecosystem that nurtures future litigators, arbitrators, and policymakers through high-impact training, competitions, and capacity-building programs.

Dr. Mishra holds a Ph.D. in Law from Maharashtra National Law University, Nagpur. He is a Gold Medalist in LL.M. and earned his B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) from National Law University and Judicial Academy, Assam. His academic excellence, coupled with practical experience in ADR, policy research, and consultancy, has made him a sought-after expert in legal education and dispute resolution. A recognized authority in mooting and advocacy training, Dr. Mishra has judged some of the most prestigious international moot court competitions. Under his leadership, IFAM is spearheading initiatives to standardize mooting practices and enhance the quality of legal education globally.

Dr. Mishra is deeply committed to bridging the gap between legal theory and practice, ensuring that students and young professionals are equipped with real-world advocacy skills. His work extends beyond IFAM, as he actively contributes to academic discourse through research publications, conference presentations, and expert-led training programs.

As President of IFAM, Dr. Mishra envisions a world where legal advocacy is a universal skill, mooting is recognized as a fundamental training ground for legal professionals, and young lawyers are empowered to navigate complex legal landscapes with confidence. Through his dynamic leadership, international collaborations, and strategic initiatives, IFAM is poised to become a global leader in legal advocacy and mooting education.

Mr. Mathuk Thumra

Vice-President, International Forum for Advocacy and Mooting (IFAM)

Mr. Mathuk Thumra is a dedicated advocate with half a decade of legal experience in Manipur and Assam. His passion for justice and strategic approach to law have made him a trusted professional in various legal domains, including criminal law, civil disputes, and constitutional matters.

As the Vice-President of the IFAM, Mr. Mathuk is instrumental in fostering a thriving environment for legal discourse, critical thinking, and the advancement of advocacy skills. His leadership has been pivotal in shaping and promoting mooting competitions that provide aspiring lawyers with the tools to sharpen their advocacy techniques and develop the confidence needed to excel in real-world litigation.

Mr. Mathuk's extensive involvement in advocacy has not only enriched his professional practice but has also empowered countless law students and young advocates to hone their skills, articulate compelling arguments, and navigate the complexities of the law. His commitment to excellence in advocacy is reflected in his continuous mentorship and guidance within the mooting community.

Driven by a deep belief in the transformative power of legal education and advocacy, Mathuk is committed to ensuring that justice is accessible to all. His work within the mooting and advocacy sphere is a testament to his dedication to shaping the next generation of legal professionals.
